About PodcastQuotes
Inspired by reddit, PodcastQuotes is an open source platform to help you discover, vote on, & share highlights from your favorite podcasts.

Eventually we'd like this system to assist in crowdsourcing full text transcripts of podcast episodes, so hearing-impaired persons can enjoy podcasts too.

PodcastQuotes is also dedicated to helping podcasters connect with fans, reach new audiences, and raise funds to sustain their show.

We want to support independent media by making it easier for podcasters to earn a living through their craft. That's why we give podcasters full control over the ads/promotions that appear on their page, and let them keep 100% of the revenue generated from these links.

PodcastQuotes is currently in beta. Please let us know how the site works for you so we can improve. Thank you and namaste!
