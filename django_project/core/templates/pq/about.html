About Podverse
Podverse is an open source podcast sharing and voting system, inspired by reddit.

Podverse strives to help podcasters reach new audiences, connect with fans, and raise funds for their broadcasts. Here podcasters can set the ads/promotions that appear on their page, and keep 100% of the revenue generated from these links, or designate them to another cause.

We'd also like this system to assist with crowdsourcing text transcripts of podcast episodes for hearing-impaired persons and researchers.
