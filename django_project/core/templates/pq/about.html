About Podverse
Inspired by reddit, Podverse is an open source platform for sharing, voting on, and highlighting podcast episodes.

Podverse's goal is to help podcasters reach new audiences, connect with fans, and raise funds for their broadcasts. Podcasters can control the ads/promotions that appear on their page for free, and keep 100% of the revenue generated from these links, or designate them to another cause.

We'd also like this system to assist with crowdsourcing text transcripts of podcast episodes for hearing-impaired persons and researchers.

Podverse is under construction. Your feedback would be appreciated.
