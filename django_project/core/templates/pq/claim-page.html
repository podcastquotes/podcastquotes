Claim Your Podcast Page
Are you a podcaster who wants to moderate your own Podverse profile page?

We give podcasters full control over the ads, promotions, and support buttons that appear on their page, and let them keep 100% of the revenue generated from these links.

If you would like to receive moderator privileges for your podcast page, please tell us by emailing contact@podverse.tv or tweeting @Podverse, and we'll help you get started. We'll just need to verify that you are the podcaster (or authorized to act on the podcaster's behalf) before making you a moderator.

If you already have a Podverse username, you can tweet us your Podverse username from your official Twitter account and we will give your account moderator privileges.

If you tweet us from your official account we can use that as verification, but if you do not have a Podverse username already we will need an email address to send you your username and password.
